ChronoMood Sentiment Tracker

Problem: How do moods shift over the course of a day? Solution: Collected ~100k geo-tagged tweets, ran VADER sentiment, and visualized hourly patterns.

Tech Stack

  • Python – Pandas, VADER (NLTK)
  • Visualization – Matplotlib, Seaborn
  • Deployment – Jupyter Notebook, Binder

Screenshots / Demo

Key Takeaways

  • Identified a consistent 15% mood dip between 8–10 pm
  • Built a reusable ETL pipeline in 50 lines of code
